Missed Connection Protection

Have you bought cheap airline tickets only to find that missing a connection has hit you where it hurts - in the pocket? Missing connections can be frustrating and incredibly expensive, but not when you take out Missed Connection Protection from Travelwaiver.

When you take out this protection and miss a connection (before you arrive at your first destination) you simply book yourself onto the next possible flight (in same cabin class) available and you will get compensated. Even if you need to check into a hotel if there are no flights on the same day we will compensate you for this. (maximum US$200 per night and room)

Features of Missed Connection Protection Protection for up to a maximum of 6 flight segments per itinerary providing they share the same booking number and minimum connection times required by the airline/s are observed.

No more stress and financial penalties from missed connections! 

Limitations:  You are reimbursed for EXTRA costs only:  meals and/or overnight accommodation at the airport or hotel near the airport (inclusive round trip transfer) if your layover is  6 hours or more. Maximum payouts of $1000.00 per person.
