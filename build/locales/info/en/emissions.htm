How can you make sure that you do not have a negative impact on the climate system and that you do not contribute to global warming? How can you do this in the most effective and fair way? The answer is simple and consists of two steps:
1) reduce your carbon footprint (your emissions of greenhouse gases).
2) compensate the rest of your carbon footprint by donating money to the most effective and ethically responsible organizations.
