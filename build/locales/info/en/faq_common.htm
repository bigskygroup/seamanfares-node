FAQ

1. How to update Passenger Information (e.g. Date of Birth, Passport Number etc.)?
> Enter *PSI or go to Information --> Secure Flight Info
If you need to modify a name BEFORE the ticket has been issued, use -1@LASTNAME/NAME where 1 is the passenger number. Then go to *PSI and update the name DOC NM ( NAME/LASTNAME ) and ER to update
2. When entering a notification into the reservation please use the following entry to have a standardized input.
> Enter 7@T/YOUR REMARK//abbreviaton
Result: 7T/YOUR REMARK//ST
3. If an airline is not in BSP Germany, check sponsored airline for ticketing.
> Enter 4F//SCT*DE-XX
XX = Airline Code e.g. A5
Result: SPONSORED CARRIERS FOR BSP GERMANY            DE

             *A5-AF/AF

You can also check with this entry : 4G/TA*XX-E

4. To keep all flight bookings up to date regarding schedule changes, everyone can work on Queue 7.
> Enter Q/7
> Update bookings with EWR
No Action is required if changes of travel times are < 3 hours BUT advise customer if he has to go earlier than expected, to the airport
Send new flight itinerary and inform passenger if changes are > 3 hours
5. How to handle reservations with flight ticket and hotel reservation?
If flight and hotel is not fraud and paid:
> Issue flight ticket as normal
> Click book hotel button > hotelshop locator will automatically appear and you will see it immediately in admin
> Change status to Issue Flights + Hotel > Appropiate E-Mail will be automatically send to passenger with hotel locator and PDF voucher included.
6. How to emulate to another PCC (3MD, 237, C1K, QV9, 086)?
> Being in WNJ Enter 4@E3MD
> To get out Enter 4@EOFF
7. How to add Travelwaiver Protection after reservation is made by passenger? Only valid and allowed on the same day of reservation is made!
> Calculate the amount of the Travelwaiver Protection and send payment link to customer > Customer need to pay immediately
> After money has been paid and received in admin, go to the bottom of the page and click the button "Travelwaiver Protection"
> Choose the right Protection and passenger and finalize with the button "Set Protection"
8. How to add an additional text to e-mails we send from admin to the customers?
> Below the drop down menu click on the link Add text email > enter the information/text (1-2 sentences max.)
> Select the status (+ email) and email will automatically send to customer
> Examples of text/information:
>> Specific airline always goes UC
>> Payment by installments fails and we ask for total payment
>> Pax booked flight from USA to Cuba
>> We half trust and ask for specific information
>> Pax is minor and we don't know
>> Child is an adult on return flight
